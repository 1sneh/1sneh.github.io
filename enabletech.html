EnableTech

Wheelchair Navigation Safety Device

Product Development and Hardware

OBJECTIVE
  • Build a reasonably-priced, reproducible edge-detecting wheelchair device that can alert the user of an approaching staircase or doorway
  • Design a solution that can be scaled up for use by other members of the disabled community
DESIGN PROCESS
  • Led complete structural re-designs for an edge-detecting wheelchair safety device using Solidworks and 3D printing
  • Increased housing capacity by 40%, doubled range of IR sensors, reduced weight, and designed efficient compartmentalized interior. Included a slot for wires
  • Added curved, ergonomic exterior
OUTCOMES
  • 3D-printed parts mount to need-knower's wheelchair and house a rechargeable battery, microcontroller (Arduino Nano), piezo buzzer, and two IR sensors
  • Redesigned the casing to allow need-knower's to easily change the battery and ensure that all components are fixed within the housing
  • IR sensors detect distance and piezo buzzers generate an auditory feedback alert.
