Undergraduate Research Experience

FLOW Lab (Experimental Fluid Mechanics)

Controllable Open-Air Flow Loop Design for Calibration of Ultrasonic Velocity Profiler (UVP)

OBJECTIVE
  • Single-Phase Laminar Flow Loop: Experimental UVP Calibration
CONTRIBUTIONS


Designed and built an open-air flow loop system to create controllable, fully-developed laminar flow for ultrasonic velocity profiler calibration and experimentation.

OUTCOMES
  • Independently designed and built a low-Reynold’s-number flow loop to calibrate and assess the performance of a UVP
  • Created consistent, repeatable flow that can be used for various other measurement applications
  • Assisted with final experimental analysis of flow
