iPhone-Based Portable Microscope

Manufacturing and Tolerancing: ENGIN 27

A portable microscope that attaches to a smartphone and doesn't require any external support. Ideal for scientists and engineers on the go!

OUTCOMES
  • Conceptualized, modeled, and manufactured iPhone case that doubly functioned as a portable microscope for field observations
  • Designed adjustable platform with rotating lens holder and specimen tray using Solidworks/AutoCAD and GD&T snap, clearance, and push fits
  • Optimized strength and durability for outdoor use. Demonstrated magnification at the final showcase
